About Us

Friends of Farmers Foundation BC is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping small and underserved farms thrive in British Columbia. We provide resources, funding, and support to strengthen the local farming community.

Our Projects

  • Providing financial grants for farm upgrades
  • Organizing workshops for sustainable farming techniques
  • Connecting farmers with local markets and buyers

How You Can Help

Your contributions make a direct impact on the lives of local farmers. Every dollar helps grow a stronger community.
